Hi, I'm Ruth!

I am a proud graduate of the University of Missouri's School of Journalism, class of 2017. I am currently working on the copy and graphics desk for The Columbia Missourian, a city newspaper in Columbia, Missouri. For the past two years, I covered the higher education beat at the Missourian. This past summer, I interned with The Kansas City Star in KCMO and wrote enterprise and general assignment stories. I'm also a history nerd, and am finishing up a minor studying classical and medieval Europe.

I'm a proud native of Oklahoma City, but I've been lucky to travel across the U.S. and the world. Last summer, I spent eight weeks teaching English as a Second Language in Sofia, Bulgaria, and also got to explore Bulgaria and Istanbul.

Some of my favorite writers are Lane DeGregory, Flannery O'Connor, Daniel Woodrell, David Halberstam and Laura Hillenbrand. You can usually find me listening to folk music, reading a novel or tramping through a state park.
